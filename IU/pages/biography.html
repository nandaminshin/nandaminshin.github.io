IU's Biography



Before debut

IU was born Lee Ji-eun on May 16, 1993, in Songjeong-dong, Seoul, South Korea. At an early age, IU became interested in pursuing a career in the entertainment industry and began attending acting classes. Soon after her elementary school years, her family's financial situation deteriorated, and they eventually moved to the nearby Uijeongbu in Gyeonggi Province. She and her younger brother lived apart from their parents in a studio room with their grandmother and cousin for over a year in conditions of great poverty. IU had little contact with her parents over this period of time but felt reassured under her grandmother's care. During her middle school years, IU found her passion for singing and decided to become a singer after being applauded for a performance she gave at her school's sports competition. She attended 20 auditions but failed all of them, and was also scammed by fake entertainment companies. IU used to train at Good Entertainment with Uee, Yubin, Heo Ga-yoon, and Jun Hyo-seong. After signing with LOEN Entertainment in 2007, she moved to Bangbae, Seoul. Despite the prospect of her being put into a girl group, she made her solo debut in 2008 after ten months of training.The name IU is a combination of 'I' and 'you' meaning "you and I become one through music".She signed with Kakao M (formerly LOEN Entertainment, now Kakao Entertainment) in 2007 as a trainee and debuted as a singer at the age of fifteen with her first mini album Lost and Found (2008).



After debut and career

Although her debut was not quite successful, the two successive albuns Growing upand IU..IM started to bring her onto the successful way. IU became porpular by the song Nagging, her very first collaborated song with 2AM Im Seulong, for the We Got Married Show, in 2010. And in the same year, Good Day which is one of the songs in Real albummade her famous countrywide. Good Day took the first place in Gaon Digital Chart for five weeks and became one of the 100 Greatest Hits of K-pop songs list of Billboard according to the 2019 records. The next albums Real+ and Last Fantasy released in 2011 also took porpularity through the whole country. After that, taking into account her angelic persona and mesmerizing voice, the princess of K-pop, IU, was given the title of “Nation's Sweetheart” and “Nation's Little Sister” after stepping into the spotlight at the young age of 15. IU officially wrote her first self-composed song Hold My Hand for the Greatest Love drama in 2011. Modern Times Album in 2013 and Flower Book Mark Remake Album in 2014 were released successively. In 2015, Chat-Shire Album, her very first album which contain onluy her self-composed songs was released. And then, Palette Album and Lilac Album also brouht a huge success for her career. IU also took part in Radio and Variety shows. IU started her acting career in 2010 as Supporting role with Teen Drama Dream High and later she appeared on the flim as a Main Lead in many other successive dramas. IU started her career at LOEN Entertainment but she moved her whole IU team from LOEN Entertainment to Edam Entertainment in 2020 January 6.
